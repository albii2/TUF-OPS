This document combines all six TUF Academy training manuals into a single comprehensive reference. It is designed for:

## THE TUF ACADEMY PRINCIPLES

These principles inform every module, every assessment, every coaching conversation:

| # | Principle | What It Means |
|---|-----------|---------------|
| 1 | **You embody the brand before you sell the product.** | How you show up — your energy, your questions, your genuine interest in the coach's program — is the first thing a coach buys. |
| 2 | **The program is the hero. TUF is the tool.** | Every conversation centers the coach's program. TUF exists to serve it. |
| 3 | **The demo proves the claim. The words anchor the memory.** | Never say a standard name without showing it. The physical action IS the brand. |
| 4 | **The 4-lane system is the first thing a coach hears and the last thing they remember.** | Lead with it. Close with it. Everything else supports it. |
| 5 | **Confidence comes from knowledge. Swagger comes from caring.** | Know your product cold. But the energy comes from genuinely caring about programs. |
