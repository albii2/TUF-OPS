# SECTION 2: THE 7 QUALITY STANDARDS

---

## QUALITY STANDARDS PHILOSOPHY

TUF has seven branded quality standards. Each is a standard TUF demands from its vendors and delivers to its athletes — not a claim to have invented the underlying material. This is exactly what Nike does with Dri-FIT (a moisture standard, not a polyester invention) and Adidas does with AEROREADY (a performance standard, not a fabric patent).

**Governing Rule:** If TUF can point to a specific, verifiable quality threshold that differentiates its product from a generic alternative, it deserves a name. If it's a universal commodity, it doesn't.

---

## STANDARD 1 — PRO CUT™

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Category** | FIT |
| **Definition** | Athletic-tapered pattern with articulated sleeves, reduced seam volume. Built to specifications derived from inside knowledge of college/pro uniform patterns. |
| **15-Second Claim** | "Athletic-tapered fit built to the specifications of what college programs actually wear. Articulated sleeves. Reduced seam volume. Not a boxy stock blank." |
| **Physical Demo** | Hold TUF jersey next to competitor jersey (or current uniform). Show taper through torso. Show sleeve articulation — they angle forward, not straight out. |
| **What Coach Sees** | The silhouette difference. The taper. The athletic proportions. |
| **Sports to Lead With** | EVERY SPORT. PRO CUT is universal. It's the first standard in every conversation. |
| **Why It's Real** | Visible and tactile. A coach can see the difference next to a generic blank. The founder's actual knowledge applied to cut specifications. |

---
