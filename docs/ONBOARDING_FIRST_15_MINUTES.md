# Onboarding First 15 Minutes
## Owner
- Verify users in /users
- Import lead CSV and assign territory/director/rep
- Review dashboard exposure + at-risk

## Director
- Open /dashboard and /territory
- Review stuck deals, stale accounts, rep accountability
- Open team opportunities and assign coaching priorities

## Rep
- Open /dashboard mission cards
- Execute next actions in /my-opportunities
- Progress near-close deals and update follow-ups

## OPS
- Open /dashboard and /orders
- Clear blockers and push vendor-ready orders
