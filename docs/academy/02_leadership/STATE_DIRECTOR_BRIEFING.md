# State Director Briefing & Expansion Overview

## Role Mandate

As a State Director, you are the leader of your state's territory operations. Your primary objective is to manage, coach, and scale a high-performing team of Territory Account Executives (TAEs) while ensuring territory data and delivery pipelines remain healthy.

## State Director Expectations

### 1. Team Health Management
You are responsible for recruiting, onboarding, and certifying the TAEs in your state. A healthy team requires:
- Conducting weekly coaching standups.
- Tracking Academy completion and certification for all reps.
- Actively assisting reps in unblocking opportunities in their pipeline.

### 2. Territory Coverage & Health
Your dashboard tracks zone and school coverage:
- Ensuring schools assigned to your reps are actively contacted.
- Monitoring territory exposure (reducing the percentage of untouched schools).
- Resolving stuck opportunities (stale > 10 to 21 days depending on pipeline stage).

### 3. Operational Controls & Margin Health
As territory manager, you must enforce critical operational boundaries:
- **Workload Limits**: Monitor rep pipelines and enforce the **25 active opportunities cap** per representative.
- **Discovery Gate**: Ensure mockup requests are not submitted prematurely without qualified discovery.
- **45% Margin Floor**: Enforce the 45% gross margin floor policy on all quotes and invoice drafts. Reps must not offer discounts unless required to close the deal.
- **Midwest Print Buffering**: Ensure that local backup textile printers are engaged for a 7-day turnaround buffer during seasonal peak times to protect referral loops.

### 4. Hiring & Recruiting Funnel
You lead the local talent funnel in your state, keeping tabs on:
- Candidate applicants.
- Scheduling and conducting interviews.
- Extending offers to new TAEs and Interns.

---

## Compensation Overview

State Directors are compensated through a combination of:
- **Base Salary**: Standard monthly executive compensation.
- **Override Commission**: A percentage of all closed-won deals generated by TAEs within your assigned state territory.
- **Performance Bonuses**: Awarded for hitting state-wide lane penetration and school coverage targets.
